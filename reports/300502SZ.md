# 300502.SZ - Eoptolink Technology
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 68/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Eoptolink is the #2 Chinese optical transceiver supplier after Innolight, with revenue exploding 179% YoY to CNY 8.65B in FY2024 as it captures share of NVIDIA's 800G orders alongside Innolight. Based in Chengdu with strong silicon photonics and LPO (linear plug optic) module capabilities, Eoptolink is the fastest-growing public transceiver company by revenue growth rate. However, it trails Innolight significantly in scale and NVIDIA relationship depth, and carries the same China geopolitical risks.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate-Strong (3.5/5)**
- #2 Chinese transceiver supplier with NVIDIA qualification. Strong SiPh and LPO capabilities.
- Scale advantage over Western competitors on cost. But trails Innolight's NVIDIA relationship depth.
- Same geopolitical/export control risks as Innolight.

### Verdict: HOLD
Eoptolink is a strong #2 to Innolight with faster revenue growth (179% vs 60%), but the $50B valuation at 22x TTM sales is steep, and the Innolight relationship with NVIDIA is deeper. If choosing one Chinese transceiver play, Innolight (300308.SZ) is the higher-conviction pick due to its dominant NVIDIA/Google share. Eoptolink is the backup trade for investors who want diversified China optical exposure.
