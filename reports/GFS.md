# GFS - GlobalFoundries
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 62/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
GlobalFoundries is emerging as Tower Semiconductor's primary competitor in silicon photonics foundry services, having acquired AMF (the largest pure-play SiPh fab outside the US) and launched the GF Fotonix platform with NVIDIA, Broadcom, Marvell, and Cisco as customers. SiPh revenue doubled to ~$200M in 2025 and is targeting $1B+ by end of decade, with a "China-free" supply chain angle that may become mandatory for US hyperscalers. The bull case is compelling but buried within a $6.5B legacy foundry business with weak 27% gross margins and declining non-SiPh segments.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate-Strong (3.5/5)**
- **GF Fotonix platform**: Established SiPh foundry with blue-chip customer base. AMF acquisition expands capacity and technology portfolio.
- **"China-free" angle**: AMF in Singapore provides a non-China SiPh supply chain -- increasingly important for US hyperscalers under export control pressure.
- **Tower Semi competition**: Tower has 85% SiPh foundry share and is expanding 5x. GFS is the #2 challenger, not the incumbent.
- **Legacy business drag**: Overall foundry margins are weak (27%) and non-SiPh segments are declining. SiPh is the growth engine but only 3% of revenue today.

### Supply Chain Relevance
- **Upstream**: Buys photonics-SOI wafers (likely from Soitec, but also potentially GlobalWafers/Shin-Etsu for non-photonics SOI)
- **Downstream**: Broadcom, Marvell, Cisco, NVIDIA fab SiPh chips on GF Fotonix, which go into optical transceivers
- **Competes with**: Tower Semiconductor (TSEM) -- the dominant SiPh foundry

### Verdict: HOLD
GFS is the #2 SiPh foundry with a credible path to $1B+ revenue, but the legacy foundry business and weak margins make it a diluted play on the photonics theme. At 3.8x sales with SiPh revenue doubling annually, there's optionality value if photonics becomes a larger share. But Tower Semiconductor (TSEM) is the higher-conviction SiPh foundry play with 85% share and dedicated expansion. GFS is the diversified alternative for investors who want foundry exposure with SiPh upside.
