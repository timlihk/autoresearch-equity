# IPGP - IPG Photonics
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 45/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
IPG Photonics is the world's largest fiber laser company, but its business is overwhelmingly industrial (metal cutting, welding, marking) and defense (directed energy), not AI data center optics. With only 3% revenue growth in FY2025 and no meaningful data center customer base, IPG is a photonics company in name but not an AI photonics beneficiary. The new defense laser business (CROSSBOW) is interesting but won't move the needle near-term.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate (2.5/5)**
- World leader in fiber lasers by volume. Strong technology and vertical integration. But the industrial laser market is commoditizing with aggressive Chinese competition (Raycus, JPT).

### Catalysts
- Q4 rebound (+17% YoY) suggests possible cyclical bottom
- CROSSBOW defense laser system
- Potential tariff benefits vs Chinese competitors

### Risks
- Industrial laser commoditization -- Chinese competitors closing the gap
- No AI data center exposure
- 3% annual growth is stagnant
- Gross margins declining on tariffs and competition
- $5.2B market cap on $31M net income = 167x P/E

### Verdict: AVOID
IPG Photonics is a great technology company in the wrong market for this thesis. Industrial fiber lasers are commoditizing, not growing, and the company has no meaningful AI data center business. The defense pivot is interesting but early. For AI photonics exposure, every other name in this universe is more directly relevant. Skip unless you're specifically bullish on industrial laser recovery.
