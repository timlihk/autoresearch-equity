# 0522.HK - ASMPT
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 81/100
## Memory Chokepoint Tier: Oligopoly with Moat

### Thesis
ASMPT is one of the best undercovered memory-supercycle names because it sits inside the advanced-packaging bottleneck that turns HBM dies into usable AI memory. Its TCB platform is already winning repeat orders across HBM and logic, and the company says its HBM4 12H solutions were the first to secure orders from multiple HBM players. This is a less obvious but potentially more durable way to own the cycle than just buying memory wafers.

### Value Chain Position
ASMPT sits in the packaging-equipment layer:
- thermo-compression bonding for HBM and advanced logic
- hybrid bonding and chip-to-wafer tools
- broader advanced-packaging tooling used by OSATs, foundries, and memory makers

If these tools do not ship, HBM packaging throughput becomes the bottleneck even when memory dies are available.

### Supply Chain Map
**Upstream:** high-precision motion, optics, process modules, and ASMPT's own advanced-packaging R&D.

**Downstream:** foundries, OSATs, and HBM memory players.

**Competitors:** Hanmi, Hanwha, and other packaging-tool suppliers depending on the specific process step.

**Displacement risk:** customer insourcing, tool-share competition, or slower-than-expected HBM-capacity additions.

### Moat Analysis
ASMPT's moat comes from technology depth and customer embedment:
- it said in March 2026 that TCB achieved record revenue in 2025
- management targets 35-40% TCB market share
- it claims HBM4 12H TCB orders from multiple players and is already working on HBM4 16H
- the company says it has the largest installed base in TCB, surpassing 500 tools worldwide

This is a genuine packaging choke point, not just a broad semi-cap bet.

### Catalysts
1. HBM4 12H orders from multiple players are already secured.
2. Through 2025, the company kept winning repeat orders in both advanced logic and HBM-linked TCB flows.
3. AI demand should keep driving advanced-packaging spend across both memory and logic.
4. The market still treats ASMPT as a broad equipment name rather than a targeted HBM enabler.

### Risks
1. Advanced packaging remains a competitive space with multiple credible vendors.
2. Some non-core businesses can dilute the pure HBM story.
3. Gross margin has been uneven as mix shifts.
4. Tool demand can be lumpy quarter to quarter.

### Verdict: BUY
ASMPT is one of the most interesting undercovered bottlenecks in the project. It offers direct exposure to the HBM4 packaging bottleneck with a stronger installed-base moat than most investors realize.

### Price Target: HK$130
That target assumes the market gives more credit to ASMPT's HBM4 and TCB position as 2026 orders convert into revenue and backlog quality improves.
