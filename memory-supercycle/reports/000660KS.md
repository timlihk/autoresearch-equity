# 000660.KS - SK hynix
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 88/100
## Memory Chokepoint Tier: Duopoly Chokepoint

### Thesis
SK hynix is the highest-conviction stock in the memory supercycle because it leads the most constrained part of the market: HBM. The company exited 2025 with record financials, record margins, and explicit claims that it is the only supplier able to ship both HBM3E and HBM4 stably at the same time. As AI moves from training toward broader inference, SK hynix also gains a second leg from server DRAM and Solidigm-backed enterprise SSD demand.

### Value Chain Position
SK hynix controls the highest-value fabrication layer in AI memory:
- HBM for AI accelerators
- server DRAM and modules
- enterprise SSD and QLC storage through its NAND portfolio and Solidigm

This is where the current memory bottleneck lives. If HBM cannot ship, the accelerator stack cannot scale.

### Supply Chain Map
**Upstream:** advanced DRAM nodes, packaging lines in Cheongju and Indiana, tool suppliers, and testing infrastructure.

**Downstream:** NVIDIA and other accelerator customers, hyperscalers, server OEMs, and enterprise SSD customers.

**Competitors:** Micron and Samsung in HBM; broader competition in NAND, but SK hynix remains unusually strong in AI memory.

**Displacement risk:** Samsung narrows the HBM gap faster than expected, or AI accelerator demand pauses.

### Moat Analysis
SK hynix has the strongest practical moat in AI memory today:
- HBM revenue more than doubled in 2025
- HBM4 large-scale production was already underway by January 2026
- management said 2026 DRAM and NAND demand had already been fully committed
- the company couples HBM leadership with broader server DRAM and eSSD demand

This is a rare combination: the leading bottleneck supplier is also printing the highest margins in the industry.

### Catalysts
1. HBM4 leadership is already in production, not just sampling.
2. Customer demand for 2026 memory output is already spoken for.
3. 321-layer QLC and Solidigm eSSD expand the story beyond HBM.
4. New packaging facilities in Korea and the U.S. extend supply-chain control.

### Risks
1. HBM leadership attracts aggressive competitive responses from Samsung and Micron.
2. The stock has already rerated hard, so expectations are high.
3. Korea and U.S.-China technology tensions remain a constant background risk.
4. If AI capex shifts from explosive growth to digestion, the multiple could compress quickly.

### Verdict: BUY
SK hynix is the clearest way to own the bottleneck at the center of the AI hardware stack. It deserves the highest score in this project.

### Price Target: KRW 1,250,000
That target assumes the market continues to reward the HBM leader with premium multiples while margins and capacity visibility remain far above historic memory-cycle norms.
