# Scoring Criteria

Total score out of 100, weighted across 5 dimensions.

## 1. Business Quality (25 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 21-25 | Wide moat, dominant market position, secular tailwind, best-in-class management |
| 16-20 | Clear competitive advantage, growing market share, strong management |
| 11-15 | Decent business, some differentiation, competent management |
| 6-10  | Commodity business, limited moat, average management |
| 1-5   | Declining moat, losing share, governance concerns |

## 2. Financial Strength (25 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 21-25 | High margins, strong FCF, low debt, high ROIC (>20%), consistent growth |
| 16-20 | Above-average margins, positive FCF, manageable debt, good ROIC (15-20%) |
| 11-15 | Average margins, breakeven FCF, moderate leverage, decent ROIC (10-15%) |
| 6-10  | Thin margins, negative FCF, high leverage, low ROIC (<10%) |
| 1-5   | Burning cash, overleveraged, deteriorating fundamentals |

## 3. Growth (20 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 17-20 | Revenue CAGR >20%, accelerating growth, large TAM runway |
| 13-16 | Revenue CAGR 10-20%, steady growth, good TAM |
| 9-12  | Revenue CAGR 5-10%, mature but stable |
| 5-8   | Revenue CAGR 0-5%, slow growth, limited upside |
| 1-4   | Declining revenue, shrinking TAM |

## 4. Valuation (20 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 17-20 | Significantly undervalued vs intrinsic value and peers, PEG <1 |
| 13-16 | Moderately undervalued, reasonable multiples for growth |
| 9-12  | Fairly valued, priced for current expectations |
| 5-8   | Somewhat overvalued, priced for perfection |
| 1-4   | Extremely overvalued, bubble territory |

## 5. Catalyst & Timing (10 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10  | Strong near-term catalyst, positive momentum, sentiment inflection |
| 7-8   | Identifiable catalyst within 6 months |
| 5-6   | No clear catalyst, neutral sentiment |
| 3-4   | Headwinds present, negative sentiment |
| 1-2   | Major overhang, regulatory/legal risk, earnings deterioration |

## Verdict Thresholds

| Score | Verdict |
|-------|---------|
| 75+   | BUY — strong conviction, set a price target |
| 55-74 | HOLD — decent business, not compelling at current price |
| <55   | AVOID — fundamental or valuation concerns |

## Adjustments

- **+3 bonus** if insider buying in last 90 days
- **+2 bonus** if recent earnings beat + raised guidance
- **-3 penalty** if accounting red flags or restatements
- **-5 penalty** if under SEC/regulatory investigation
